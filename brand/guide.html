#1 / Foundation

Every floor. One star.

Dance Star is a marketplace: dancers find classes, socials, and instructors; studios find rooms full of beginners. It launches in Phoenix on the back of Phoenix Bachata Dance and is built to outgrow it. The brand is designed for the champion stage and worn casually in the crackpot stage, so nothing needs a rebrand on the way up.

Positioning

For dancers who want more nights on the floor, Dance Star is the one place every class, social, and instructor in the scene shows up. Studios run their brands; Dance Star runs the room.

Personality

Magnetic like a Friday social, beginner-obsessed like the academy that built it, credible enough for studios to trust it with bookings and revenue.

Voice

"See you on the dance floor."

Warm, direct, second person. Always reassures beginners they belong. Never corporate, never breathless.

The star motif

A five point star is the shared thread across every palette and every future sub-brand: the rating, the wordmark separator, the map pin, the loading spinner. One shape doing four jobs beats four decorations.

#2 / Brand Architecture

Three models, one path

Parent brand or sub-brand is a timing question, and the answer changes as Dance Star grows. One visual law holds in every model: Dance Star never wears Phoenix Bachata red (#E12330). Adjacency, never inheritance.

Start here

Endorsed launch

Stage 01 / Now

Dance Star leads with its own name and palette. PBD appears as a text endorsement only, never as color or logo on Dance Star surfaces. Borrows trust with zero visual debt.

DANCE STAR
"from the team behind
Phoenix Bachata Dance"
Grow into

Sibling system

Stage 02 / Challenger

Dance Star becomes the platform brand; PBD becomes its founding listed studio with permanent "Founding Studio" placement. Separate palettes, shared star motif.

DANCE STAR (platform)
├ Phoenix Bachata Dance ★
├ Studio B ├ Studio C ...
End game

The flip

Stage 03 / Champion

Dance Star is the parent. Studios adopt "a Dance Star studio" and keep their local colors, PBD red included. The platform palette stays untouchable underneath them all.

Phoenix Bachata Dance
a DANCE STAR studio

#4 / Typography

Type does the dancing

One display face with real personality, one workhorse, one label voice. All free on Google Fonts, so studios and contractors can reproduce everything without licenses. Hierarchy comes from size and slant, never from adding more families.

Archivo Black ItalicDisplay / headlines, wordmark, posters. Uppercase, tight tracking (-0.03em), sizes 40 to 160px.
Every floor. One star.
InterBody / UI copy, descriptions, forms. 400 to 700, 14 to 18px, generous line height.
Friday's social starts with a 9 PM beginner class. No partner needed, no experience needed. Grab a spot and we will see you on the dance floor.
Space GroteskLabels / eyebrows, buttons, data, timestamps. 500 to 700, uppercase, letter spacing +0.1 to +0.24em.
FRI JUL 10 · 9:00 PM · PROVISION · 7 SPOTS LEFT

  • 02Adopt the endorsed launch model now. Dance Star surfaces carry its own palette plus one text line crediting Phoenix Bachata Dance. Revisit the sibling model when a second city goes live.
  • 03Reskin the live site. Swap the current PBD-red Dance Star site to the chosen system; type stack changes to Archivo Black Italic + Inter + Space Grotesk.
  • 04Commission the wordmark. Lock the star separator and a proper logo lockup once the palette is settled; then extend to IG templates, class flyers, and the booking flow.
